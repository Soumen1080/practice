Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered!

How do I register as a coach?

To register as a coach, click on the "Register" button on the homepage and fill out the required information.

How can students find coaches?

Students can search for coaches by sport, location, and availability using our search feature.

Is there a fee for using the platform?

Registration is free for both coaches and students. However, some premium features may require a subscription.

Can I change my profile information later?

Yes, you can update your profile information at any time by logging into your account and navigating to the profile settings.

How do I contact support?

You can contact our support team through the "Contact Us" page or by emailing support@example.com.
